# Benchmark Acceptance Criteria

A benchmark result must meet the following to be accepted:

## Required Fields

- classical baseline
- dataset name
- version
- claim status
- limitations

## Quality Checks

- Same dataset used for classical and quantum
- No data leakage
- Metrics clearly defined
- Reproducible configuration

## Claim Checks

- Claim matches delta vs classical
- No unsupported advantage claims
- Limitations are clearly stated

## Rejection Conditions

A benchmark may be rejected if:

- No classical baseline is provided
- Results are inconsistent or unverifiable
- Claims exceed evidence

## Goal

Ensure every benchmark contributes valid, comparable, and honest data.
